Magic the Gathering deck-builder

Site Purpose with Goals

This will be for those who want to see their magic decks at a glance and have the utility to edit their decks virtually.

Some of the tools that users may find here is;

  • The number of cards permitted in a given game mode
  • The card 'colors' being used in each deck
  • Suggestions for how to use the various mechanics
  • The abilty to easliy search up cards they do and don't have
  • Easliy share their created decks with friends

Target Audience

  • How do I make a good deck?
  • What are some tips and tricks to deck building?
  • What sets are 'Standard legal'?
  • What else could I try to have more fun with my decks?

People who have been collecting Magic the Gathering cards over the years may be looking for some tool that can assist them with keeping track of all of them. Or, those who are new to the game may not know how to put a deck together so they can have fun with their friends while still feel like they made the deck themselves (instead of their friend creating it for them). This website will benefit both types of players.
